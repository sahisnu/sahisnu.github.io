Hi!

I am an AI Research Scientist at the Multimodal Dialogue and Interaction (MDI) group at Intel Labs, USA and work on Human-AI collaboration and dialogue & interaction systems research. Prior to joining Intel, I obtained my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), USA under the supervision of Prof. Bing Liu, completed my Masters in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) - Roorkee, India under the supervision of Dr. Dhaval Patel (presently, Researcher at IBM TJ Watson Research Lab, NY) and Bachelor in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST) - Shibpur, India.

My research works broadly falls into the area of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Deep Learning, Natural Language Understanding (NLU), Dialogue and Interactive Systems, Lifelong and Continual Learning, Open-World AI / Learning problems. My current research topics include Knowledge Base Reasoning, Natural Language Interface (NLI) Design, Open-world Interactive Learning, Factual and Commonsense Reasoning, and Sentiment Analysis.

During my PhD, I also worked as a Summer Research Intern at Microsoft Research - Redmond on Natural Language Interaction (NLI) system design and at Futurewei Technologies (aka Huawei Research USA) on project related to user activity & interest mining. Before joining UIC and after completion of my masters, I also worked as a Junior Research Fellow at IIT Roorkee under the supervison of Dr. Dhaval Patel on News Data Analytics and Location-aware Review Mining projects.

Research Professional Services

Hi!

I'm a final year PhD student of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), USA. My advisor is Prof. Bing Liu . Prior to joining UIC, I completed my Masters in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) - Roorkee, India under the supervision of Dr. Dhaval Patel (presently, Researcher at IBM TJ Watson Research Lab, NY) and obtained my Bachelor in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology (IIEST) - Shibpur, India.

My research works broadly falls into the area of Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, Data Mining and Text Mining and AI Reasoning problems. My current research topics include Lifelong Learning, Dialogue and Interactive Systems, Knowledge base Reasoning, Question-Answering and Sentiment Analysis.

During my PhD, I worked as a Summer Research Intern at Microsoft Research - Redmond on Natural Language Interaction (NLI) system design and at Futurewei Technologies (aka Huawei Research USA) on project related to user activity & interest mining. Before joining UIC and after completion of my masters, I also worked as a Junior Research Fellow at IIT Roorkee under the supervison of Dr. Dhaval Patel on News Data Analytics and Location-aware Review Mining projects.
